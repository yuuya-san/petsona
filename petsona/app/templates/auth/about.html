About PetSona

PetSona is a smart web-based platform built to promote responsible pet ownership and help reduce pet abandonment. It bridges the gap between pets and owners by encouraging better understanding, guided care, and informed decision-making that strengthens their long-term bond.

In addition to intelligent pet-related guidance, PetSona connects users to nearby pet hotel services that offer daycare and overnight care. This ensures that pet owners can focus on work, travel, or daily responsibilities with peace of mind, knowing their pets are safe, cared for, and comfortable in their temporary stay.

Our Mission

To promote responsible pet ownership through intelligent guidance, education, and accessible care services. We aim to reduce pet abandonment and strengthen the bond between pets and owners by encouraging awareness, preparedness, and long-term commitment.

Our Vision

A future where every pet is properly understood, cared for, and placed in environments that match their needs—reducing abandonment and strengthening human–animal relationships through technology-driven solutions.

Our Core Values

  • Care — Prioritizing the well-being of both pets and owners
  • Responsibility — Promoting informed and lifelong commitment to pet care
  • Trust — Delivering honest and transparent guidance
  • Innovation — Using technology to improve pet care, matching, and services
